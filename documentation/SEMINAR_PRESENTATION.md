# Seminar Presentation: AI-Driven Music Streaming System

**Topic:** Building an AI-Driven Music Streaming & Recommendation App
**Project Name:** Music-Flow

---

## 1. Introduction (Hook)
**Speaker 1:**
Good morning everyone! Today, our team is excited to present our project: **Music-Flow**, an AI-Driven Music Streaming and Playlist Recommendation System. 


## 2. Our Solution
**Speaker 3:**
We built a web application from scratch. Users can log in, play their favorite songs, and most importantly—get **automatic, personalized song recommendations** based on their unique listening history!

## 3. Key Features of Our App
**Speaker 1:**
Here is what our application can do:
- **User Accounts:** Secure registration and login.
- **Premium Music Player:** A sleek, modern "Midnight Serenity" themed player with a premium glassmorphic design and real-time audio visualization.
- **Smart Matrix Recommendations:** Our logic looks at your most-played songs, identifies your favorite genres and moods, and suggests the best tracks for you automatically.
- **Dynamic Search & Filters:** Easily find songs by typing the name or filtering by mood (e.g., Happy, Sad, Chill) and genre.

## 4. Technology Stack (How we built it)
**Speaker 2:**
We combined several beginner-friendly yet powerful technologies:
- **Frontend:** HTML5 & CSS3. We designed a responsive UI with dynamic moving backgrounds and animated components.
- **Backend:** Python with the Flask framework. Python handles our server, routing, and recommendation logic.
- **Database:** MySQL. We use it to store user credentials, song metadata, and track how many times a user plays a song.

## 5. How the "AI" Engine Works (Simply Explained)
**Speaker 3:**
Our recommendation engine uses a clever algorithmic approach:
1. **Tracking:** Every time you hit 'play', the database records your action.
2. **Analysis:** The system checks which songs you played the most recently.
3. **Pattern Matching:** It extracts the **Genre** and **Mood** of your top heavily-played songs.
4. **Delivery:** It searches the database for *other* unplayed songs that match those exact parameters and serves them seamlessly to your "Recommended Picks" section!
